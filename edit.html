EDITORIAL







Dr. H.S. Saini

Chief Editor

We, at Guru Nanak Institutions, derive a great deal of pleasure in publishing the eight volume of Journal of Innovation in Electronics and Communication success. The issue 1 of volume 8 is now being published with the increase in the acceptance of this journal, three issues per year are now been planned. The crux objective of this publication is to give a platform for academicians, young researchers and professionals to share their state of the art knowledge and research initiatives. The journal has motivated research community in making contribution to the quality of this journal all over the globe. The editorial team is working tirelessly to maintain the praiseworthy innovativeness and excellence in the field of Electronics and Communications.

The present Issue 1 Volume 8 is brought out with highly informative & suggestive articles with original research papers covering the latest technologies. All research papers contributed to the journal are subjected to multiple peer blind reviews by external and internal experts from the field and final review and clearance is done by our Editorial Advisory Board, so as to preserve originality and novelty in their content. Every effort has been made to make this journal conform to the international standards and to keep up academic interest among the stake holders.

We now look forward to the research community, academic peers and professional stalwarts from all parts of the world to strengthen our resolve to be leaders in this field of engineering / academic journalism by contributing articles and research papers of high standards. We further thank all our contributors and reviewers for helping us bring this issue of the journal with high level quality papers.

We welcome suggestions and feedback from the readers of the journal so as to bring useful improvement in the content and quality of the journal.
With best wishes,

Dr. H. S. Saini
Chief Editor
