Abstract

Medical imaging is a technique used for representing internal body organs, bones etc., It also helps in understanding the functionality of organs or tissues in the body. In order to diagnose or treat patients with their illnesses, in order to know the exact problem of the patient based on the symptoms, many doctors go for medical imaging.

Keywords

Medical imaging, X-Ray, Filtering Technique, Butterworth high pass filter, Offset filter, Histogram equalization
